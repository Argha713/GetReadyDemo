SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 Released, Download Hall Ticket PDF

  • Admin
  • Oct 16, 2024

The Staff Selection Commission has released the SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 for all regions. Candidates who have successfully submitted the SSC MTS 2024 Application Form will be able to download their SSC MTS 2024 Admit Card through the regional SSC website. In this blog, we have provided the SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 download link, the process for downloading the MTS and Havaldar Admit Cards 2024, and more.

SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 Out

The SSC MTS 2024 Computer-Based Test (CBT) has begun, and the last exam will be held on 14th November 2024. The exam is being conducted in three shifts each day. More than 35 lakh (3.5 million) candidates have applied for 9,583 vacancies for Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) and Havildar positions. The SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 has already been released for all nine regions. Candidates can download their hall tickets from their respective regional SSC websites by entering their registration number and date of birth.

How To Download the SSC MTS Call Letter 2024?

Candidates who will be appearing in the examination must be aware of the downloading procedure of the admit card. We have mentioned below the detailed steps to download the SSC MTS Hall Ticket 2024.

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to the regional SSC website links available on the official SSC website to access your admit card.
  2. Find Admit Card Section: On the SSC homepage, click on the “Admit Card” link to go to the regional website where you applied.
  3. Locate Admit Card Notification: On the regional site, look for the notice that says “STATUS / DOWNLOAD ADMIT CARD FOR Multi Tasking Staff Examination (Tier-1), 2024 (TO BE HELD 30th September to 14th November 2024)” and click on it.
  4. Enter Login Details: Enter your Roll Number/Registration ID and Date of Birth/Password as provided during registration.
  5. View Admit Card: Your SSC MTS Tier 1 Admit Card will appear on the screen.
  6. Download and Print: Download the admit card and print it, as you must bring it to the exam center.

What to Do if You Forget Your MTS Roll Number?

If you forget your SSC MTS Roll Number or Registration ID, follow these steps to recover it:

  • Visit Official Website: Go to the regional links on the site to access the admit card.
  • Find Admit Card Section: Click on the “Admit Card” link on the SSC homepage to reach the regional website for your application.
  • Locate Admit Card Notification: On the regional site, look for the notice titled “STATUS / DOWNLOAD ADMIT CARD FOR Multi Tasking Staff Examination (Tier-1), 2024 (TO BE HELD 30th September to 14th November 2024)” and select it.
  • Enter Your Name: Provide the name used during registration.
  • Provide Your Father’s/ Mother’s Name and Date of Birth: Enter these details as registered.
  • Download and Print: Download the admit card and print it, as you’ll need to bring it to the exam center.

What To Do After Downloading The SSC MTS Admit Card 2024?

Candidates appearing for the SSC MTS Tier 1 Exam 2024 must carefully check the details on their admit card to ensure everything is correct. Below are important points to keep in mind regarding the admit card and exam day:

  1. Verify Details: Check your name, exam venue, and exam date on the SSC MTS Admit Card 2024.
  2. Report Errors: If any details are incorrect, contact the authorities immediately for corrections.
  3. Follow Instructions: Carefully read and follow the instructions given for the exam.
  4. Carry ID Proof: Make sure to bring a valid ID proof with a clear photo and signature on the exam day.

SSC MTS Admit Card 2024 – Provisional Acceptance

Provisional acceptance means a candidate’s application is accepted based on initial information but is not fully confirmed until further checks are completed. Candidates must ensure they meet all requirements throughout the process.

  1. Provisional Acceptance: Application is accepted based on provided details; full confirmation occurs after checks, ensuring all criteria are met.
  2. Candidate Responsibilities: Ensure educational qualifications, work experience, age, physical, and medical requirements meet standards.
  3. Post-Examination Checks: Documents are reviewed after the exam; additional certificates may be requested, and unmet requirements can result in disqualification.
  4. Document Verification: Educational qualifications and caste/category certificates are verified during the Document Verification stage.
  5. Physical and Medical Standards: Assessed by relevant departments once exam results are announced.
  6. Submission of Certificates: Submit necessary documents when requested by departments or organizations; failure to do so may lead to disqualification.

Misprints or Errors on SSC MTS Admit Card 2024

If a candidate notices any errors or discrepancies on the SSC MTS Hall Ticket 2024 PDF, they should promptly contact the relevant authorities. The commission will address the issue and provide a new admit card with the necessary corrections. Ensuring corrections is crucial, as the examiner will carefully examine the admit card during the exam. Therefore, it is recommended to thoroughly review the admit card after downloading it and promptly address any errors or misprints.
