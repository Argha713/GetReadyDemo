Eligibility Criteria for IRDAI Assistant Manager Exam 2024

It is an essential requirement for all interested aspirants to first ensure they meet the eligibility criteria set by IRDAI. The Age limit of the candidate as per the eligibility criteria says, they should be between 21 to 30 years of age. In addition, they should have a Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university with at least 60% marks as of the last day of the application process.

  • Age Limit: 21 to 30 years
  • Educational Qualification: Bachelor’s degree from a recognized university
