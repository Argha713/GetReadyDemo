UIIC AO Syllabus 2024: United India Insurance Company Limited (UIIC) has updated the syllabus and exam pattern for the Administrative Officer (AO) post. The new UIIC AO Syllabus 2024 covers subjects like English language, reasoning, quantitative aptitude, general awareness, and computer literacy. Candidates should also review the latest exam pattern to understand how the marks are given, the maximum marks possible, and how the questions are formatted.

UIIC AO Exam Pattern 2024

The UIIC AO Exam Pattern 2024 is detailed and comprises five main sections Reasoning, English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, and Computer Literacy, along with General Awareness to assess Technical and Professional Knowledge. The following is an overview of the Exam Pattern based on the UIIC Notification PDF 2024:

  1. Nature of Examination:
    • The UIIC AO Examination 2024 is conducted as an Online Computer-based Examination in an Objective Type format.
  2. Language of Examination:
    • The UIIC AO Examination 2024 will be conducted in both English and Hindi languages, except for the test on the English Language.
  3. Marking Scheme:
    • For every correct answer, candidates will be awarded 1 mark.
  4. Negative Marking:
    • There will be a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer.
  5. Unanswered Questions:
    • No marks will be deducted for unanswered questions.
  6. Time Duration:
    • A total duration of 2 hours is allocated for the exam.

This information is crucial for candidates preparing for the UIIC AO Exam, as it provides a comprehensive understanding of the exam structure and evaluation criteria outlined in the UIIC Notification PDF 2024.
