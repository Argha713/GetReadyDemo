UIIC AO Cut Off 2024, Category Wise Previous Year Cut Off Marks

  • Admin
  • Oct 14, 2024

United India Insurance Company Limited (UIIC) has released 200 vacancies for the position of Administrative Officer (Generalist and Specialist) on its official website, www.uiic.co.in. The cut-off marks represent the minimum scores candidates need to secure in order to move on to the next phase of the selection process. Those who score above the cut-off marks will qualify for the interview round. You can refer to this article for the section-wise and category-wise cut-off marks from previous UIIC Administrative Officer (AO) exams.

Steps to download UIIC Administrative Officer Cut Off

To download the UIIC Administrative Officer Cut Off, follow these steps:

  • Visit the official website of UIIC at www.uiic.co.in.
  • Navigate to the Recruitment section and click on “Category wise, Section wise cut off-Recruitment AO Examination 2024”.
  • Upon clicking, the Cut Off will appear on your screen.
  • Check the Cut Off according to your category and download the PDF.

Factors Affecting UIIC AO Cut Off

Various factors crucially influence the UIIC AO Cut Off 2024 in determining the minimum qualifying marks for candidates. These factors comprise:

  • Number of Vacancies: Available vacancies directly impact the cutoff. Higher vacancies may lower the cutoff, while fewer vacancies could raise it.
  • Reservation Policy: The policy, including quotas for categories like SC, ST, OBC, and EWS, affects category-wise cutoffs. The cutoff may vary based on the number of candidates applying under each category.
  • Difficulty Level of the Exam: The exam’s complexity significantly influences the cutoff. A more challenging exam may lower the cutoff to accommodate difficulty.
  • Number of Candidates Appearing: Total candidate turnout influences the cutoff. More candidates may lead to increased competition and a higher cutoff, while fewer candidates could result in a lower cutoff.
