UIIC AO Recruitment 2024, Notification Out for 200 Posts

  • Admin
  • Oct 14, 2024

The United India Insurance Company Limited (UIIC) has announced the UIIC AO Recruitment 2024 to fill 200 vacancies for Administrative Officer posts across various disciplines. This recruitment drive is an excellent opportunity for those aspiring to establish a career in the insurance sector. The online application process begins on 15 October 2024 and remains open until 05 November 2024. Candidates are advised to thoroughly review the eligibility criteria, selection process, and other essential details before applying.

UIIC AO Notification 2024

The UIIC AO Notification 2024 has been officially released by the United India Insurance Company Limited, inviting applications for 200 Administrative Officer (AO) posts across various disciplines such as Generalist, Legal, Risk Management, and more. This recruitment drive provides a remarkable opportunity for candidates aspiring to enter the insurance sector. Interested applicants must review the eligibility criteria and other important details before applying, as the online exam is scheduled for 14 December 2024. With a competitive salary of approximately ₹88,000 per month, this is a career-defining chance for many.

UIIC AO Notification 2024

UIIC AO Recruitment 2024

The UIIC AO 2024 Recruitment offers a stable and rewarding career opportunity in the insurance industry. With competitive pay and a wide range of roles, including generalist and specialist positions, this recruitment drive should not be missed by eligible candidates.

UIIC AO Vacancy 2024

The UIIC AO Recruitment offers a total of 200 vacancies, split across various disciplines such as Generalists, Legal, Risk Management, Finance, and others. The breakdown of vacancies is as follows:

UIIC AO Apply Online 2024

The application process for UIIC AO Recruitment 2024 is entirely online. Eligible candidates can apply through the official website of UIIC from 15 October 2024. The last date for submitting the application form and paying the application fees is 05 November 2024. Ensure that all required documents are submitted and the form is completed correctly to avoid disqualification.

UIIC AO Recruitment 2024: Eligibility Criteria

Before applying for UIIC AO Recruitment 2024, candidates must ensure that they meet the specific eligibility criteria, which include educational qualifications and age requirements. The eligibility varies by post, so it’s crucial to check the details carefully.

Educational Qualifications for UIIC AO 2024

Discipline Qualification Required
Generalists Graduation/Post Graduation in any discipline with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST) from a recognized university.
Legal Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Law with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST). Experience as a practicing lawyer is preferable.
Risk Management B.E./B.Tech with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST) and Post Graduate degree or PGDM in Risk Management.
Finance & Investment Chartered Accountant (ICAI) or Cost Accountant (ICWA), or B.Com. with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST), or M.Com. from a recognized university.
Automobile Engineers B.E./B.Tech in Automobile Engineering with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST), or M.E./M.Tech in Automobile Engineering.
Chemical/Mechatronics Engineers B.E./B.Tech in Mechatronics or Chemical Engineering with 60% marks (55% for SC/ST), or M.E./M.Tech in the same discipline.
Data Analytical Specialists B.E./B.Tech in Computer Science/IT/Statistics/Data Science, or related fields, or MCA/Postgraduate in relevant disciplines. Knowledge of Power BI and RDBMS is preferred.

Age Limit for UIIC AO Recruitment 2024

The minimum age limit to apply for UIIC AO Recruitment 2024 is 21 years, and the maximum age is 30 years. Age relaxation is applicable as per government norms for candidates belonging to reserved categories.

Conclusion

The UIIC AO Recruitment 2024 offers a golden opportunity for candidates aspiring to build a career in the insurance sector. With 200 vacancies available and a handsome salary, this recruitment is an excellent step towards a promising future. Candidates must ensure that they meet the eligibility criteria and apply within the specified dates. Start preparing now for the online exam scheduled on 14 December 2024, and take your career to new heights with UIIC! Keep visiting the official website for updates and application details.
