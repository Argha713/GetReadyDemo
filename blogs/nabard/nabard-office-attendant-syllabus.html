NABARD Office Attendant Syllabus 2024, Exam Pattern

  • Admin
  • Oct 16, 2024

NABARD is set to conduct an online examination in two phases to recruit eligible candidates for the Office Attendant position under Group C. With 108 vacancies announced, this is a valuable opportunity for candidates who have completed their 10th grade. The selection process will include two stages: an Online Exam (Preliminary and Main exams) and then a Language Proficiency Test (LPT). You can find the detailed NABARD Office Attendant Syllabus 2024 and Exam Pattern in the following sections.

NABARD Office Attendant Syllabus 2024

NABARD has released an official notification announcing 108 vacancies for the Office Attendant post. The selection process includes two online tests, followed by a Language Proficiency Test (LPT). To excel in the first stage, candidates must be familiar with the NABARD Office Attendant Syllabus 2024 and the exam pattern. Understanding the syllabus helps candidates prioritize key topics, making preparation more efficient. Key details regarding the NABARD Office Attendant 2024 Syllabus and Exam Pattern are summarized in the table below.

NABARD Office Attendant Selection Process 2024

The selection process for the NABARD Office Attendant 2024 includes three stages- Prelims, Mains, and LPT. The LPT is a qualifying stage where the final merit list will be based on the performance of the candidates in the Prelims and Mains Exam.

  • Online Test (Preliminary Exam and Mains Exam)
  • Language Proficiency Test (LPT)

NABARD Office Attendant Exam Pattern 2024

To become an Office Attendant at the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), candidates must go through two stages: Prelims and Mains. Both exams are crucial for selection, but the Prelims is a qualifying exam, while the Mains determine the final merit.

NABARD Office Attendant Prelims Exam Pattern 2024

The Preliminary exam is the first step in the selection process. While it is essential to pass the Prelims to move on to the Mains, the marks scored in this stage are not considered for the final selection. Key details of the Prelims exam include:

  • The exam is objective in nature.
  • Each correct answer is awarded 1 mark.
  • There is a negative marking of 0.25 marks for each wrong answer.
  • The exam is conducted online.

NABARD Office Attendant Mains Exam Pattern 2024

Candidates who clear the Prelims will move on to the Mains exam, which is critical for final selection. The marks obtained in the Mains will be considered for the merit list. Important points to note:

  • The Mains exam consists of objective-type questions.
  • There is a penalty of 0.25 marks for each incorrect answer.
  • The Mains exam results determine the final merit list.
