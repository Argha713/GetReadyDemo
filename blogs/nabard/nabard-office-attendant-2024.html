Steps to Apply for NABARD Group C Recruitment 2024

To apply for the NABARD Office Attendant positions, follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Visit the official website – Go to the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development’s website at https://www.nabard.org/.
  2. Navigate to Career Notices – On the homepage, click on “Career Notice,” and then proceed by clicking “Click Here to Continue.”
  3. Access the Application – Under “Recruitment to the post of Office Attendant in Group ‘C’ service- 2024,” click ‘Apply Here.’
  4. Register as a new user – If you are applying for the first time, click “Click here for New Registration” and provide your name, contact details, and email to create your registration ID and password.
  5. Log in to your account – Use your registration ID and password to log in.
  6. Fill out the application form – Enter your details carefully and verify all information before submitting.
  7. Upload documents – Upload your photograph, signature, and left thumb impression as per the specified dimensions:
    • Photograph: 200 x 230 pixels (20kb-50kb)
    • Signature & Thumb Impression: 140 x 60 pixels (10kb-20kb for signature; 20kb-50kb for thumb impression)
  8. Handwritten Declaration – Submit a handwritten declaration: “I, (Name of the candidate), hereby declare that all the information submitted by me in the application form is correct, true, and valid. I will present the supporting documents as and when required.”
  9. Payment – Complete the payment process using your preferred method.

Ensure that all details are accurate and documents meet the specifications before final submission.

NABARD Office Attendant Eligibility Criteria

To apply for the NABARD Office Attendant Recruitment 2024, candidates must meet specific educational and age criteria. Applicants are required to provide certificates as proof of their qualifications, and those who do not meet these criteria will not be considered for the recruitment process.

Educational Qualification

Candidates applying for the NABARD Office Attendant position must have completed the following educational requirements:

  1. 10th Standard (S.S.C./Matriculation): Applicants must have passed the 10th Standard from a recognized board in the State or Union Territory to which they are applying. The certificate should be issued by the state/regional office of NABARD.
  2. Ex-servicemen Category: For candidates belonging to the Ex-servicemen category, the requirement is a minimum of 10th Standard (S.S.C./Matriculation) qualification. Additionally, they must have rendered at least 15 years of defense service, provided they have not completed a degree outside the Armed Forces.

It is essential to note that candidates holding Graduation or higher qualifications are not eligible to apply for the NABARD Group C Recruitment 2024.

Moreover, the educational qualification (10th Standard) must be completed and the result declared before 1st December 2024 to be considered for this recruitment.

Age Limit (as of 1st October 2024)

The age criteria for NABARD Office Attendant posts require candidates to fall within a specified age range:

  • Minimum Age: 18 years
  • Maximum Age: 30 years

This means applicants should be born between 2nd October 1994 and 1st October 2006 (both days inclusive). Additionally, there are age relaxations for certain categories, as follows:
