Examination Tips

Achieving success in any exam is not just about how well a candidate has prepared but also hinges on maintaining the right temperament in the examination hall. At GET READY, we strive to create an environment that mirrors real exam conditions, helping students manage pressure and anxiety effectively. It's essential for candidates to know how to tackle exam stress so they can perform at their best. We encourage our students to master the fundamental theories of different subjects, which will provide them with a deeper understanding of each topic.

To enhance general awareness, candidates should make a habit of reading newspapers, and magazines, and watching news channels daily. Engaging in discussions on current affairs with peers and mentors can also be highly beneficial in staying up-to-date and refining their knowledge.

Below some exams tips are given:

  • Revise the topic as many times as possible & extract the difficulties & problems & solve them by discussing with teachers in classroom.
  • Always participate in the unit test & chapter wise test conducted by us time to time as per test schedules.
  • One day before examination don’t do too much study, try to keep fresh & energetic.
  • Before starting solving the question, read all general instruction carefully.
  • Try to solve those questions first which can be answered easily. Tough questions should be tried after it.
  • Always try to keep focus on time & accuracy.
  • There is no need to answer a question if you don’t know how to solve it. By doing this you can get rid of negative marking.
  • Always try to keep cool & calm during examination in any situation, no need to be panic if things are not going well.
  • Reach the examination hall at least one hour before the scheduled timing & keep one water bottle with you.
  • Overall you must take care of your health.
